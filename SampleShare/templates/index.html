About OceanShare

This site was built for the Local Hack Day at CU Boulder in less than eight hours by a group of (previously) random strangers:

  • Alex Goodwin
  • Jared Jolton
  • Philip Chow
  • Jessica Masson
  • Karthik Kannan
  • Henna [Last name?]

The goal is to create a service that allows people from all over the Internet to share music samples. Think of it as a cross between FreeSound and Splice - amazingly awesome.
